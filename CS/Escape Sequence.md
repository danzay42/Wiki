[[AWK]] Sequence | Meaning
--- | ---
`\b` | backspace
`\f` | form feed
`\n` | newline (line feed)
`\r` | carriage return
`\t` | horizontal tab
`\v` | vertical tab
`\c` | any other character `c` literal (or mask symbol `\`)
`\ddd` | octal value `ddd`, where `ddd` is 1 to 3 digits between 0 and 7
